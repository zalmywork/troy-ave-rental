Privacy Policy

Troy Ave Rental · last updated June 2026

This policy explains how Troy Ave Rental ("we") handles information collected through our booking website and notification system.

Information we collect

  • Guests: name, email, phone number, and booking details that you provide when requesting a booking or messaging us.
  • Staff: the mobile number and email a staff member enters into our private admin settings to receive operational notifications.

How we use it

Solely to operate the rental: to process and manage booking requests, communicate with guests about their stay, and notify the owner and cleaner about bookings and messages. We do not sell or rent personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

SMS / text messages

Phone numbers used for SMS notifications belong to internal staff who entered them themselves and consented to receive transactional alerts. We do not share these numbers with third parties for their own marketing. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. See our SMS Opt-In Policy.

Service providers

We use trusted services to run the site and deliver messages (e.g., hosting, email, and SMS providers). They process data only to provide their service to us.

Data retention & your choices

We keep booking and contact information only as long as needed to operate the rental. You may request that we delete your information, and staff may remove their number from the admin settings at any time.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the property owner through our booking site at troy-ave-rental.vercel.app.
