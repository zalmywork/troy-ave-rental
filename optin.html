SMS Notifications — Opt-In Policy

Troy Ave Rental · short-term rental operations · last updated June 2026

Troy Ave Rental (operated by Somerset Street Inc / Kook) operates a single short-term vacation rental. We use a toll-free phone number to send transactional SMS notifications to our own internal staff — the property owner and the cleaning person — so they can manage reservations and turnovers. This page documents how those recipients consent to receive messages.

Who receives messages

Only internal staff: the property owner and the assigned cleaner. We do not message guests, consumers, or the general public by SMS, and we never use purchased or third-party contact lists.

How recipients opt in (web form)

Each recipient personally enters their own mobile number into the rental's private, PIN-protected admin dashboard (the "Notifications" settings) and checks an explicit consent box directly beside the phone field. No number can receive texts unless its owner entered it and checked this box themselves. This is the exact consent control shown to each recipient at the point of opt-in:

Cleaner phone (for SMS)
+1 555 000 0000
I agree to receive operational SMS text notifications from Troy Ave Rental at the number above (new booking requests, guest portal messages, and booking confirmations). Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help. See our SMS Policy and Privacy Policy. Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

The host opts in the same way, via an identical consent box beside the host phone field. The box is unchecked by default; consent is only recorded when the recipient checks it.

Verbal consent script

Consent is obtained verbally using the following script:

"We'd like to send you text notifications from our toll-free number when a guest submits a booking request, sends a message through the booking portal, or when a booking is confirmed — so you can manage reservations and schedule turnover cleanings for the Troy Ave rental property. Message frequency varies based on booking activity. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Do you agree to receive these text notifications?"

What messages we send

Strictly transactional operational alerts, triggered by activity in the booking system:

  • A new booking request is submitted
  • A guest sends a message through the booking portal
  • A booking is confirmed (so the cleaner can schedule the turnover)

No marketing, advertising, or promotional content is ever sent.

Sample message

New booking: Jane Smith, 2026-06-10 to 2026-06-13 at Troy Ave Rental. Add the turnover to your calendar.

Message frequency, rates, HELP & STOP

  • Frequency: low volume — typically only a few messages per month, tied to actual bookings.
  • Rates: message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile plan.
  • Opt out: reply STOP at any time to stop messages, or remove your number in the admin settings.
  • Help: reply HELP for assistance.
