SMS Notifications — Opt-In Policy

Troy Ave Rental · short-term rental operations · last updated June 2026

Troy Ave Rental operates a single short-term vacation rental. We send transactional SMS notifications to our own internal staff — the property owner and the cleaning person — so they can manage reservations and turnovers. This page documents how those recipients consent to receive messages.

Who receives messages

Only internal staff: the property owner and the assigned cleaner. We do not message guests, consumers, or the general public by SMS, and we never use purchased or third-party contact lists.

How recipients opt in (verbal + self-entry)

  • Each recipient verbally agrees to receive operational text alerts.
  • They then personally enter their own mobile number into the rental's private, PIN-protected admin settings (the "Notifications" section of the management dashboard).
  • No number is added without the owner of that number entering it themselves.

What messages we send

Strictly transactional operational alerts, triggered by activity in the booking system:

  • A new booking request is submitted
  • A guest sends a message through the booking portal
  • A booking is confirmed (so the cleaner can schedule the turnover)

No marketing, advertising, or promotional content is ever sent.

Sample message

New booking: Jane Smith, 2026-06-10 to 2026-06-13 at Troy Ave Rental. Add the turnover to your calendar.

Message frequency, rates, HELP & STOP

  • Frequency: low volume — typically only a few messages per month, tied to actual bookings.
  • Rates: message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile plan.
  • Opt out: reply STOP at any time to stop messages, or remove your number in the admin settings.
  • Help: reply HELP for assistance.
